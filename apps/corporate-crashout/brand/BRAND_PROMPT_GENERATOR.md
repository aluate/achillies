# Brand Brief Prompt Generator for AI

Use this prompt with your AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) to help fill out your brand brief.

## Copy & Paste This Prompt:

```
I need help creating a comprehensive brand brief for my futures trading education platform called "Corporate Crashout Trading."

Here's what my platform does:
- Subscription-based futures trading education platform
- Offers 3 tiers: Engine A Basic, Engine A Live, and Engine A Complete
- Provides TradingView indicators for NQ futures trading
- Includes live trading sessions (2x per week for higher tiers)
- Educational content: lessons, videos, documents hosted on Google Drive
- Private Discord community access
- 1-on-1 trading review sessions (add-on)
- Domain: corporatecrashouttrading.com

Target audience:
- NQ futures traders (both new and experienced)
- People looking to improve their trading skills
- Traders who want professional-grade indicators
- Those who value live guidance and community

Brand personality I'm aiming for:
- Professional but approachable
- Confident but not arrogant
- Educational and helpful
- Modern and tech-forward
- Focused on results and trading performance

Please help me fill out a brand brief with:

1. **Brand Purpose** - A clear statement of what Corporate Crashout Trading is and why it exists

2. **Positioning** - A one-line positioning statement

3. **Color Palette** - Suggest a professional color scheme suitable for a trading platform with:
   - Primary colors (2-3 colors with hex codes)
   - Secondary/accent colors (2-3 colors with hex codes)
   - Usage notes for each

4. **Typography** - Recommend font pairings that work well for:
   - Headlines/display text
   - Body text and UI
   - Suggest Google Fonts or web-safe alternatives

5. **Taglines** - Create 3-5 approved taglines that convey:
   - Professional trading education
   - Results-focused approach
   - Community and support

6. **Website Hero Lines** - Write compelling hero headline and subheading for the homepage

7. **Visual Identity** - Describe the visual style that would work:
   - Design aesthetic
   - Imagery suggestions
   - UI/UX style recommendations

8. **Tone & Voice** - Define the brand voice with:
   - 3-5 adjectives that describe how the brand communicates
   - Words/phrases to use
   - Words/phrases to avoid

9. **Do's and Don'ts** - Brand guidelines for:
   - What aligns with the brand
   - What to avoid

Please make the recommendations professional, modern, and suitable for a serious trading education platform while remaining approachable and not intimidating to newer traders.
```

---

## Alternative: Step-by-Step Prompts

If you prefer to work through sections one at a time, use these individual prompts:

### For Color Palette:

```
Help me create a professional color palette for Corporate Crashout Trading, a futures trading education platform. I need:

1. Primary colors (dark backgrounds, professional)
2. Accent colors (for CTAs, highlights, data visualization)
3. Hex codes for each color
4. Usage guidelines

The brand should feel professional, modern, trustworthy, and tech-forward. Think trading dashboard aesthetic.
```

### For Typography:

```
Recommend a typography system for Corporate Crashout Trading, a futures trading education platform. I need:

1. A display/headline font (for hero sections, main headlines)
2. A body/UI font (for content, buttons, navigation)
3. Font pairing that works well together
4. Suggestions from Google Fonts

The typography should feel professional, modern, readable, and suitable for financial/trading content.
```

### For Taglines:

```
Create 5 taglines for Corporate Crashout Trading, a futures trading education platform. The taglines should:

- Convey professional trading education
- Emphasize results and performance
- Appeal to both new and experienced NQ futures traders
- Be memorable and impactful
- Work well for marketing and website hero sections

Make them professional but not boring, confident but not arrogant.
```

### For Hero Copy:

```
Write homepage hero section copy for Corporate Crashout Trading, a subscription-based futures trading education platform. I need:

1. Main headline (compelling, action-oriented)
2. Subheading (explains what the platform offers)

The copy should:
- Be clear about what the platform does
- Appeal to NQ futures traders
- Emphasize value and results
- Include a call-to-action direction

Keep it professional but accessible, confident but not hype-y.
```

### For Tone & Voice:

```
Define the tone and voice for Corporate Crashout Trading, a futures trading education platform. I need:

1. 5 adjectives that describe how the brand communicates
2. Examples of words/phrases to use
3. Examples of words/phrases to avoid
4. Short example sentences showing the tone

The voice should be professional, educational, confident, and helpful - like a knowledgeable trading mentor who's approachable and results-focused.
```

## How to Use

1. Copy the main prompt above and paste it into your AI assistant
2. Review the suggestions your AI provides
3. Copy the sections you like into `BRAND_BRIEF.md`
4. Refine and customize as needed
5. Complete any sections the AI didn't cover
6. Review the full brief for consistency

## After Creating Your Brand Brief

1. Fill out `brand/BRAND_BRIEF.md` completely
2. Update the status from "Template" to "Complete"
3. Share with your development/design team
4. Use it as a reference for all marketing materials
5. Update as your brand evolves
